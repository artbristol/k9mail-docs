Removing an Account

To remove an email account from K-9 Mail do the following:

  1. Go to the accounts screen
  2. Long-press the name of the account you want to remove. A menu pops up.
  3. Select the option "Remove account"
  4. You're asked to confirm the removal of the account. Click "OK" to confirm.
